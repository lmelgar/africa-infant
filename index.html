The saddest record

According to UNICEF, an estimated of 5.9 million children under five years old will die in 2015. This means that every minute, 11 children will die. Most of them, will die in Africa. Although most of the countries have decreased their infant mortality rate within the last decade, Sub-Saharan Africa faces a big challenge: the continent's rate is 15 times the average for developed regions. Angola has the saddest record. Among the causes, two of them top the saddest record: diarroea and pneumonia.
